About Us

The Refugee Learning Accelerator is a project of the MIT Media Labâ€™s Learning Initiative. We support engineers and computer scientists from the Middle East to create technology for refugee learners through training, mentorship and funding. The Learning Initiative is part of the MIT Media Lab[hyperlink], an anti-disciplinary research lab at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology that designs technologies for people to create a better future. The Learning Initiative[hyperlink] is focused on the future of learning, which we believe should be playful, project-based, peer-focused and passion-fuelled.
