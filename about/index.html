What We Do

We support engineers and computer scientists from the Middle East to create educational technology for refugees. We provide training, mentorship and funding to teams through a six month program.

Who We Are

The Learning Initiative is part of the MIT Media Lab, an anti-disciplinary research lab at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology that designs technologies for people to create a better future. The Learning Initiative is focused on the future of learning, which we believe should be playful, project-based, peer-focused and passion-fuelled.
