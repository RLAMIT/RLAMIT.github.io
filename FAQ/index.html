General Questions

Why the focus on learning?

Because it’s what we do! The MIT Media Lab Learning Initiative is focused on the future of learning; we develop technologies and workshops centered around the four Ps of creative learning: peers, play, passion and projects. We’re keenly aware that a “lack of educational opportunities” in the Middle East is one of primary "push" factors cited by refugees arriving in Europe. And that as the war in Syria continues, the crisis around education in the region is only going to become more acute.

What do you mean by "refugee learners?"

We’re interested in supporting projects which benefit refugee youth, aged 15-24, who remain in the Middle East, and want to learn--whether that is in a formal school system or more informally. The refugee learner does not necessarily have to be the end user of the project--it could also be parents, teachers etc., but all projects should be beneficial to refugee learners in some capacity.
